SILVER is the name for the point system monitored by Tullbot that tracks how many points you have per account. You can use silver to get prizes like steam games and Rocket League keys. You earn 1 silver every 3 minutes or so when a stream is live. Following the channel gets you 1,000 silver. There are more than a few ways to earn silver over and above just watching the channel.
